Australian Business Visas allow business people to go to Australia to invest and/or set up a business in Australia.


Subclass 188 – Business Innovation and Investment Visa

This visa is for successful business owners or investors from overseas who are willing invest in new or existing business in Australia or make designated investment in Australian State or Territory government bonds.The Business innovation and investment visa involve a two-stage application process.At the first stage business migrants apply for a provisional visa and then if they meet certain criteria, they apply for a permanent visa.


Visa The visa includes three streams:

Business Innovation stream (subclass 188A)

For people with business skills who want to establish, develop and manage a new or existing business in Australia.

Investor stream (subclass 188B)

For people who want to make a designated investment of at least AUD $1.5million in an Australian state or territory and maintain business and investment activity in Australia.

Significant Investor stream (subclass 188C)

(See attached Brochure for our high yield investment products) For people who are willing to invest at least AUD $5million into complying investments in Australia and want to maintain business and investment activity in Australia.


Subclass 132 – Business Talent Visa

This is a permanent visa designed for high calibre business people who are owners or part owners of an overseas business and have a genuine and realistic commitment to participate in the management of a new or existing business in Australia.

The Australian Student visa programme provides international students the opportunity to study in Australia at all levels of education, including primary, secondary, tertiary, postgraduate, vocational and non-award courses. International students require a student visa before commencing any course or study in Australia.


Australia School Student Visa

This visa enables international students to study at school in Australia. This applies to students applying for a first or subsequent student visa or joining a family member who is on a student visa. The type of institute/course should be: a primary school course or a secondary school course, including junior and senior secondary or - an approved secondary school exchange programme.


High Education Student Visa

International students who have been accepted to study in a registered bachelor degree, associate degree, graduate certificate, graduate diploma or masters by coursework are required to hold an Australian Higher Education Student visa.
