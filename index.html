Property Management

At Found Group we are dedicated to providing property owners with the best Property Management Services available in Melbourne. Better property management means greater returns from your investment, and greater peace of mind for landlord. Our care and commitment to managing your property ensures for your higher rental returns, less vacancies, better tenants and lower overheads. In Addition, we also provide short term management services to expand your options and increase your investment returns. Our short management service includes the below features;

High Rental Guarantee

We provide a rental guarantee higher than the market rate with different options to cater for your needs.

$0 Property Management Fee

At Found Group, we provide a professional and complimentary property management service. No management fees or hidden costs.

Interior Styling & Photography

With our professional interior designer onboard, we provide modern styling of your property and complimentary professional photography to capture your property at its best.

Hosting, Preparation & Maintenance

We will take care of the entire property management process. This includes guest enquiries, key drop-offs, cleaning services, and ongoing maintenance all free of charge.
